- **Tertiary:** Create a resource hub for current clients (login portal)

## 2. TARGET AUDIENCE

### 2.1 Primary Persona: "Stuck Ambitious"
**Demographics:**
- Age: 20-35 years old
- Gender: Primarily male (80%), but program open to all
- Location: Cleveland metro area (expandable to Ohio, then national)
- Income: $20K-50K/year
- Credit Score: 500-650
- Debt: $5K-25K
- Education: High school to some college

**Psychographics:**
- Feels stuck financially, frustrated, "left behind"
- Sees success online (influencers, entrepreneurs) and wants it
- Hates current job but doesn't know how to escape
- Has ambitions/ideas but no capital or credibility
- Willing to work hard, just needs guidance and accountability
- Resonates with "do you want a higher credit score or do you want to be known for something?"

**Pain Points:**
- Bad credit prevents apartment approvals, car loans, job opportunities
- Living paycheck to paycheck, no savings
- One emergency away from financial disaster
- Feels like success is for "other people"
- Doesn't trust banks, financial advisors, or traditional systems
- Wants to start a business but has no foundation

**Goals:**
- Fix credit to unlock opportunities
- Save $10K+ for emergency fund and future
- Increase income 20%+ through better job or side hustle
- Start a business or personal brand
- Prove to themselves (and others) they're capable of more
- Get in shape, build discipline, transform identity

### 2.2 Secondary Persona: "Pre-Entrepreneur"
- Already has side hustle or business idea
- Needs business credit and funding
- Likely to upsell to business services after graduation

## 6. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

### 6.1 Platform/Stack

**Recommended Platform: Webflow**
- Pros: No-code, fast to build, great SEO, hosting included, CMS built-in
- Cons: Monthly cost (~$18/month), limited backend logic
- Best for: Speed to market, easy to update, professional design

**Alternative 1: WordPress + Elementor**
- Pros: More flexible, cheaper hosting, massive plugin ecosystem
- Cons: Requires more maintenance, security concerns, slower
- Best for: Budget-conscious, need advanced functionality

- Pros: Maximum control, best performance, scalable

## 11. RISKS & MITIGATION

**Risk 1: Low Website Traffic**
- Mitigation: Invest in paid ads, aggressive content marketing, local partnerships

**Risk 2: High Discovery Call No-Show Rate**
- Mitigation: SMS reminders, email reminders 24 hours before, offer incentive (free guide if they show)

- Mitigation: Improve call script, address objections better, offer payment plans

- Mitigation: Weekly accountability, gamification (belt system), community support

**Risk 5: Negative Reviews/Testimonials**
- Mitigation: Set clear expectations, under-promise and over-deliver, handle complaints quickly
