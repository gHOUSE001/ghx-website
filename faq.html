Results

Hit all 8 graduation metrics (720+ credit, $10K saved, 20% income increase, etc.) and you get $500 cash or $1,200 in GHX credit toward future services. It's our skin in the game. We only win when you win. It's a stretch goal—not everyone hits all 8—but those who do get rewarded.

Most clients see 30-50 point credit gains in the first 3 months from credit-building products reporting. Dispute removals add quick jumps. By Month 6, you're typically 80-120 points up. The full 150-200+ point increase usually happens by Month 9-12. Savings and income growth happen in parallel.

You still walk away in a much better position than where you started. Most clients who do the work hit most of the 8 metrics. The guarantee is for hitting all 8—it's ambitious. But even if you hit 5 or 6, you've transformed your finances.
